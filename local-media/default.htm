Local Media

  • Objectives and Strategy
  • Media Events

Objectives

  • To create visibility and attention

  • To generate awareness about our activities

  • To provide visibility for donors/sponsors

Strategy

  • To keep the local media posted on activities

  • To invite local media to attend events, football schools etc.

  • To secure an ongoing dialogue between the national Branch Offices and the media and between the local school leaders and the local media
