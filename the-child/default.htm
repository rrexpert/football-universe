Objective

  • To promote children’s grassroots football accordingly with our specific “fun football concept” and the basic principles of “Sport for all”.

  • To provide all children regardless of gender, talent, social, political and religious differences with a possibility “to live football” in a football club with good friends and under adult guidance.

  • To promote equality of sexes

Strategy

  • To involve as many children as possible in our Open Fun Football Schools.

  • To ensure that minimum 50% of all children at Open Fun Football Schools are children that are not yet members of a local football club, but children who would love to play football.

  • To involve a minimum of 25% girls at each Open Fun Football School
