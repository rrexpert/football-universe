Schools

  • Objectives and Strategy
  • Current Projects

Objectives

  • To encourage local schools to organise grassroots football outside class hours

  • To promote the incorporation of the praxis and values of Open Fun Football Schools in the national school curricula

Strategy

  • To recruit minimum 40 percent school teachers as voluntary leaders and coaches

  • To develop school sports programs in cooperation with selected schools and municipalities

  • To ensure that the Ministry of Youth and Sport is on the CCPA national advisory boards
