Cooperation Agreements in the Balkan Region

We have signed cooperation agreements with the Ministries of Youth and Sports in most of the countries. They all take active part in our advisory boards (please see below). Further in Bosnia Herzegovina we have jointly implemented a mine-awareness program during our football schools.

In Macedonia we are implementing a joint school sport program (please see above) and it is the ministry that imports all equipment needed for the football schools. Further the ministries lent the project support when we approach the municipalities.
